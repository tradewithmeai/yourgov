# Ethics Guardrails

YourGov explains public records. It does not judge personal character.

## Non-negotiables

- No "best/worst MP" moral ranking.
- No implication that absence equals guilt or disinterest.
- No claim that donations/interests prove corruption.
- No "broke promise" language without source-linked pledge evidence.
- No political recommendation engine.

## Required caveats

- Record-based: "This shows recorded action, not intent."
- Coverage caveat: "Data may be incomplete or delayed."
- Match caveat: "If mapping confidence is low, do not claim certainty."

## UI and wording rules

- Prefer neutral wording: "recorded vote", "no recorded vote", "source-linked".
- Avoid accusatory framing.
- Label mock/demo data clearly.

## AI explain mode

- AI output must inherit source constraints.
- AI should provide "what this does not prove" language where relevant.
